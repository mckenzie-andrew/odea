You can write SQL, you can optimize a Python script, and you can spin up a Spark cluster. But when the job is slow, do you know why? When the database hangs, do you know what the disk is doing? When the network times out, do you understand the packet flow?

This course peels back the layers of abstraction. We stop pretending that the Cloud is a fluffy nebula of infinite resources and admit what it really is: **a collection of physical machines with physical limits.**
